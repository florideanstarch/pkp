## Present State

We came across this [paper](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.06.26.661135v2) at work, which is trying to model cellular behaviour upon perturbation of a gene using transformer architecture. The aim is to predict the whole transcriptome of cells given a gene perturbation. 

A human cell consists of roughly 20,000 protein coding genes, expression of these genes is through a complex interplay of events that unfold under tight regulation. The identity of a cell, after all, depends on this.

We are attempting to understand this method and will update the blog once we have something solid.
