I was part of a bootcamp at work that took me through the fundamentals of AI/ML over a few months. This page is my attempt to record what I learned while studying core ML and DL concepts. The goal was to understand these ideas well enough and to familiarise myself with the code. Eventually, I would be required to apply these methods to problems in biology. I followed the resources mentioned below and took detours into prerequisites whenever needed.

Now, I’ve given myself a year to work through the additional material on this page, that was not covered during the bootcamp.
